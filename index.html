Who are we?

We are Cycle Cymru, based in Torfaen Principal Area we are the most popular cycling group that takes a positive and friendly approach to those would want to get into cycling.

Though cycling isn't nearly as dangerous as driving and emits practically no carbon deposits, there is still dangers to it, such as improperly riding or riding irresponsibly on the main road where you risk being involved in a moter vehicle accident, to prevent this type of accident ever occuring we offer training programs for those who require to learn to ride safely and be able to ride next to others in a unionised pattern.

If your looking to get involved, drop by our store on the Torfaen High Street and sign up, we will provide you with a leaflet aswell as take your phone number to update you on any future notices or any promotions and events.

Have fun riding!
